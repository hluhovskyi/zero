# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: May 11, 2026*

**Zero** is a personal finance tracking app developed by Artem Hluhovskyi.

## Data Collection

Zero does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All financial data you enter — accounts, transactions, categories — is stored locally on your device only.

## Third-Party Services

Zero does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Storage

Your data is stored exclusively on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact: hluhovskyi@gmail.com
